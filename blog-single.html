Improve design with typography?

Improving design with typography involves a combination of art and technique.

Recognize that most modern fonts come as part of a set of related treatments, which can help develop unity in your designs.Use a limited number of font families to maintain a strong visual hierarchy. A practical limit is often three families.

Avoid distorting the shape or natural dimensions of a font. Fonts are designed with specific proportions for a reason.

Adjust the spacing between characters to avoid awkward gaps and ensure readability.Use different font sizes and styles to guide the reader’s eye and establish a reading order.Grid systems can create hidden harmonies in your layouts, making your design more cohesive.

Pay attention to the contrast between your text and its background to ensure legibility.Sometimes a project demands a human touch. Hand lettering can add personality and uniqueness to your design.Remember, typography is not just about making words legible; it’s about using type to create an experience and evoke emotions. Practice and experimentation are key to mastering typography in design
